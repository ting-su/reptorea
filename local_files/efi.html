Our Vision
Our Mission

Building Inner Capacity for the AI Era

We are entering an era of unprecedented technological power. EFI focuses on human flourishing. We champion and support educational initiatives that cultivate attention, emotional resilience, and ethical judgment—equipping the next generation with the inner capacity to thrive alongside AI.
Core Pillars

A Holistic Approach

01. Cognitive Resilience

In an era of infinite information, attention is our scarcest resource. We support work on metacognition and attention skills that helps people stay clear, grounded, and able to choose what truly matters amid constant digital noise.

02. Ethical Agency

Technology does not choose; people do. We support approaches that weave empathy, ethics, and long-term responsibility into how children and adults learn, lead, and make decisions with AI.

03. Future Ecosystems

Building living bridges between research labs and learning communities—connecting scientific insight with real-world educational settings and the people who shape them.

Research Focus

Science & Inquiry We Support

EFI serves as a convening and support platform for research. We collaborate with and support researchers, educators, and institutions whose work explores questions such as:
  • Neuroplasticity How contemplative, reflective, and relational practices shape developing brains, resilience, and long-term well-being.
  • Digital Wellbeing Empirical models of attention, habit formation, and recovery in environments saturated with algorithmic feeds and screen-driven demands.
  • AI & Human Values Human-centered, value-aligned ways of bringing AI into learning, professional judgment, and collective decision-making.
Frontier Themes We Track
  • Directed Neuroplasticity Countering the passive wiring of algorithms with self-directed structural changes in the brain that support deep focus and agency.
  • Cognitive Immunology Building the “mental immune system” — cultivating resistance to algorithmic addiction, information overload, and manipulation.
  • AI Alignment & Ethics Preparing the human-in-the-loop to address alignment challenges and to keep powerful AI systems anchored in human judgment and wisdom.
